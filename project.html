Digital Directory FSKTM(Prototype)

The FSKTM Digital Directory is a prototype designed to provide easy access to important information about the Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology (FSKTM). This directory helps students, staff, and visitors navigate faculty resources, contact details, and department information in a user-friendly way.

  • Back End: Laravel
  • Front End: BootStrap
  • Database: MySql, PHP
