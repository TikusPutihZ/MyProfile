My Interest

Reading Comics

Reading comics is a captivating way to experience storytelling through a blend of art and dialogue. Whether it's action-packed superhero adventures, heartwarming slice-of-life tales, or deep fantasy epics, comics offer a unique and immersive reading experience. With vibrant illustrations and engaging narratives, they bring stories to life in a way that traditional books cannot. From classic Western comics to Japanese manga and Korean manhwa, this medium continues to evolve, providing endless entertainment and inspiration for readers of all ages.

Omniscient-reader Viewpoint(Click the picture to direct to the comic webpage)

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint (ORV) is a manhwa based on a web novel of the same name. It follows Kim Dokja, an ordinary office worker who has been the sole reader of a web novel called Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse for years. One day, the novel's world becomes reality, and since he is the only one who knows the story's events, he must use his knowledge to survive and change the fate of the world.

Playing Games

Gaming isn't just a hobby—it's a portal to endless worlds, thrilling adventures, and strategic challenges. Whether diving into vast open worlds, testing reflexes in fast-paced battles, or crafting intricate stories through choices, gaming fuels creativity, problem-solving, and social connections. From retro classics to cutting-edge virtual reality, every game offers a unique experience, making it a dynamic and ever-evolving passion. Whether played solo for immersive storytelling or with friends for cooperative fun, gaming is more than entertainment—it's an art, a skill, and a way of life.

Zenless Zone Zero(Click the picture to direct to the official game webpage)

Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ) is an upcoming action RPG developed by HoYoverse. Players take on the role of a Proxy, a guide who helps others navigate the dangerous Hollows filled with monsters and enemies. The game features fast-paced combat, a stylish cyberpunk aesthetic, and a diverse cast of characters with unique abilities.
